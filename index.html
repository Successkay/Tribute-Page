Nelson Mandela

1918 – 2013

Biography

Nelson Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid activist, lawyer, and politician who served as President of South Africa from 1994 to 1999. He was the country’s first Black head of state and the first elected in a fully representative democratic election.

  • 1918: Born in Mvezo, South Africa
  • 1962: Imprisoned for 27 years
  • 1990: Released from prison
  • 1994: Became President
